Miso Soup Recipe

You make miso happy!

Calories: 40 Calories from fat: 14 Total fat: 2 grams Saturated fat: 0 grams Trans fat: 0 grams Cholesterol: 0 grams Sodium: 0.86 grams Total carbohydrates: 3 grams Dietary fiber: 0 grams Sugars: 0 grams Protein: 3 grams

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Dashi granules and miso paste
  • Tofu
  • Green Onions

Procedure

  1. Bring the water and dashi granules to a boil.
  2. Reduce the heat and whisk in miso paste.
  3. Stir in the tofu and green onions.
  4. Simmer and serve.
